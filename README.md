**How to import Zarba Resourcepack**

1. Open your Minecraft
2. Click `Option`
3. Click `Resource Packs`
4. Drag the Zarba Resourcepack into the window or click `Open Pack Folder` and copy the Zarba Resourcepack to that folder.
